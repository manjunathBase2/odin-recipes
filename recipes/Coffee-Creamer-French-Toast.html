Coffee Creamer French Toast

Description

This recipe for coffee creamer French toast is a great way to use up leftover coffee creamer. It’s a simple recipe that requires only a few ingredients and a few minutes of your time. The result is a sweet and creamy French toast that’s perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Coffee creamer French toast is a great way to add a little extra flavor to your breakfast routine. It’s a simple and delicious recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  1. 4 slices of bread
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 1/4 cup coffee creamer
  4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  6. Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

    Step 1

  • In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, coffee creamer, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.

    • Step 2

  • Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a little butter or oil.

    • Step 3

  • Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.

    • Step 4

  • Place the bread in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.

    • Step 5

  • Repeat with the remaining slices of bread.

    • Step 6

  • Serve the French toast warm with your favorite toppings.
